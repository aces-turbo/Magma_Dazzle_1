The Magma-Dazzle Story

Art is my escape, just like the molten magma burst out to escape suppression within the earth crust. Putting myself in a position to see beauty whiles preserving originality was an inner desire since my school days. School didn’t give me the opportunity to express myself in the way I thought art should be, but rather I lived in the shadow of what my lectures thought and taught just to keep my head in the game. After school the story didn’t change, I felt I was sinking and needed a place to make my own statement to the world, no matter how wrong anyone thought it was. The significance must be felt.

The desire became more as I found myself in the field of network marketing which taught me to experience a life of freedom and still get more with same effort as that of the work place. In that moment the boldness to express this desire had not gotten to me yet until my first interview for a job as an assistant architect in a firm that I later understood to be a startup.

The Founder had regretted not making a move on his entrepreneurial desires early. He saw me as a person with the potential to start a company from scratch and wanted to work with me in that capacity. Upon further reflection, after my interaction with him, I also realized I have that potential. That was the Eureka moment for me. It came to me - if someone believes in my potential and is paying me less that value, then like molten magma, I erupted from the volcano.
